Andrew Germain's PWP

I want my PWP to be my personal landing page. This page will give someone the chance to see what a website would look like with me in full control, while also allowing them to see a handful of my personal interest and hobbies. I'll occasionally post on the few social media account I have ,but I've never been too big on any social media platforms. This landing page could serve as my single social media page for potential employers, fellow collaborators and anyone else who's interested to see if who I am. My end goal with my PWP is to be a time capsule of my first personal project. One day I'll have a portfolio of all my project from now until futures present, and I can see how along.

Name: Future A. Germain Age: 32

Profession: Junior web developer at a small start-up company. This is a new company that needed help building an online presence. A small team and I work together to build the company's website.

Technology: Proficient with most technologies. Has a custom-built PC to use while working from home. Uses a macbook pro for work and portability. iPhone 20x running iOS 10.2.3.

Attitudes and Behaviors: Future Germain is extremely happy with his new position and is eager to get started. Future Germain wants to see how far he's come in his coding journey. From completing the Deep Dive Coding Bootcamp, working on small projects to hone his skill, to now landing his first coding position.

Frustrations and Needs: Future Germain is feeling a bit out of his depth and needs a pick-me-up. Future Germain needs an inspiring reminder that he's risen to the occasion before, and he can do it again. He hopes that taking a glance at his previous project will remind who is and how much he's changed over time.

Goals: Future Germain needs to get out of his rut and be motivated to go on into bigger and better things. After being motivated Future Germain will go onto writing his fist piece of code for his new promising position.

User Story: As a user, I want to be able to look at my project and see how far I've come with my coding skills and how much I've changed as a person.
