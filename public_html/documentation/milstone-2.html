Website content

My personal website will have three main pages scrolling vertically. At the top of the website will be the Navbar. The Navbar will have the title on the far left, along with my content pages, and the "contact me" all available as buttons to the right on top of the Navbar. The Navbar will stay at the top of the screen while scrolling throughout the website. At the top of the website, under the Navbar will be a horizontal filler image possibly with my name and a small bio. At the bottom of the website will contain my "contact me" section. Visitors will provide requested information and info will be directly emailed to me. Next to the questionnaire will be links to my related social media account to that visitors can click and be sent to related platform.
